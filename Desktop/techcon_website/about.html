Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots gathering of tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform attracting developers, designers, and innovators from all over the world.

From its humble beginnings in a local co-working space, TechCon now hosts tens of thousands of attendees annually, fostering knowledge-sharing and collaboration in the tech space.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we strive to empower the tech community through inclusive learning, innovative showcases, and meaningful networking. Our mission is to spark ideas that shape the future of technology and society.
